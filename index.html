01

Paper 1 — Literary Analysis

A structured, repeatable approach to any unseen text. Build the habits top-band responses rely on.

What we cover

Guiding question—unpacking what the examiner is actually asking
Textual analysis—literary & stylistic features in context
Argument structure—thesis, evidence, commentary flow
Timed practice—1h 15min (SL) / 2h 15min (HL) conditions
SL & HL Unseen Text

02

Paper 2 — Comparative Essay

Build a sharp, confident argument across two texts — structured, nuanced, and examiner-ready.

What we cover

Question analysis—selecting the strongest prompt for your texts
Comparative thesis—weaving two works into a single argument
Thematic frameworks—structure that earns marks across all criteria
Timed practice—1h 45min (SL) / 1h 45min (HL) conditions
SL & HL Comparative

03

Individual Oral

From global issue selection to polished delivery — preparation that makes the IO feel natural, not nerve-wracking.

Global issue—selecting one that's specific, arguable, and text-supported

10-min outline—structuring a focused, criteria-aligned response
Mock orals—full practice runs with recorded feedback
SL & HL Internal Assessment
